Key Idea

What Does "of the Remaining" Mean?

Some questions give you two fractions in a row. The trick? The second fraction is not of the whole — it’s of the leftover!

⚠️ "of the remaining" = a fraction of a fraction. That means multiply!
The Pattern

3-Step Method

1Find the Remainder
Subtract the first fraction from 1 (the whole).
Remainder = 1 − first fraction
2Multiply: Fraction OF Remainder
"of" means ×. Multiply the second fraction by the remainder.
Second part = second fraction × remainder
3Find What's Left
Subtract again to find the final portion.
Last part = remainder − second part
💡 "of" = multiply. This is the #1 thing to remember!
Why It's Tricky

Common Mistake

Many students see 56 and 89 and try to subtract both from 1.

❌ WRONG: 1 − 56 − 89 ← This treats 89 as part of the whole!
✅ CORRECT: The 89 is of the remaining 16, not of all the hamsters!
See It!

🐹 Kerry's Hamsters

Imagine Kerry has 54 hamsters (we pick 54 because it works nicely with 6 and 9).

1White: 56 of 54 = 45 hamsters
Remaining = 54 − 45 = 9 hamsters
2Black: 89 of the remaining 9 = 8 hamsters
89 × 9 = 8. Only 1 left!
3Yellow: 1 hamster out of 54
Fraction = 154
Hamster Grid

All 54 Hamsters

Step by Step

🚶 Solving with Fractions (No Numbers!)

We don't need to know how many hamsters Kerry has. We work purely with fractions.

1Find the remainder after white

White = 56 of all hamsters

Remaining = 1 − 56 = 16
2Find the black fraction (of ALL hamsters)

89 of the remaining are black.

"of" means multiply!

89 × 16 = 854 = 427

Multiply numerators: 8 × 1 = 8. Multiply denominators: 9 × 6 = 54. Simplify: 854 = 427

3Find the yellow fraction

Yellow = Remainder − Black

16 − 427

Common denominator of 6 and 27 = 54

954 − 854 = 154
✓Answer

154 of the hamsters are yellow.

Check Your Work

Do all parts add up to 1?

White + Black + Yellow
= 56 + 427 + 154

= 4554 + 854 + 154 = 5454 = 1 ✅
⭐ Always verify! All fractions must add back to 1 whole.

Example 1

A farm has chickens. 34 are brown. 23 of the remaining are spotted. The rest are white.

Step 1: Remaining = 1 − 34 = 14
Step 2: Spotted = 23 × 14 = 212 = 16
Step 3: White = 14 − 16 = 312 − 212 = 112
✅ Check: 34 + 16 + 112 = 912 + 212 + 112 = 1212 = 1 ✅
Example 2

A box of marbles. 13 are green. 58 of the remaining are red. Rest are blue.

Step 1: Remaining = 1 − 13 = 23
Step 2: Red = 58 × 23 = 1024 = 512
Step 3: Blue = 23 − 512 = 812 − 512 = 312 = 14
Example 3 — Three Groups!

12 are cats, 13 of remainder are dogs, 34 of the new remainder are birds. Rest are fish.

Remainder 1 = 1 − 12 = 12
Dogs = 13 × 12 = 16
Remainder 2 = 12 − 16 = 26 = 13
Birds = 34 × 13 = 312 = 14
Fish = 13 − 14 = 412 − 312 = 112
💡 When there are 3 layers of "remaining", just keep repeating the same method!
